Feat-008 | Nav — hamburger menu
  Breakpoint: 1016px (links collapse to hamburger at or below this width)
  - Logo: stays visible inside the nav, aligned left
  - Links: hidden; replaced by a hamburger icon (☰) aligned right
  - Clicking hamburger toggles a dropdown panel that drops from the RIGHT of the nav
  - Dropdown shows all links stacked vertically in a solid dark panel (min-width 200px)
  - Panel has a rounded bottom-left corner; anchored to the right edge of the viewport
  - Clicking a dropdown link scrolls to section and closes the dropdown
  - Clicking outside the dropdown also closes it
  - useState controls open/closed state; useRef handles outside-click detection
  Status: done

Feat-009 | Sticky nav — show on scroll down, hide on scroll up
  - Nav is position: fixed, top: 0, full width, high z-index
  - On scroll DOWN: nav is visible (translateY(0))
  - On scroll UP: nav slides out of view (translateY(-100%))
  - Transition: smooth CSS transition on transform (e.g. 0.3s ease)
  - useEffect + scroll event listener tracks scroll direction via lastScrollY ref
  - On mobile the hamburger dropdown also hides when the nav hides
  Status: done
